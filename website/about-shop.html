About Shop

“Moonchild” means moonlight. I named it in the hope that the accessories I create will shine like moonlight in the hands of my customers. I started this shop in 2021. One of my friends, Ichika Fujiwara, did the logo design and I created accessories, marketing, wrapping and so on. I was running the shop when I was a high school student.
